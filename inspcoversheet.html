Details of CSA B167-16 Required Safety Inspection Points

Bridge Inspection - Minimum Safety - "Frequent" Inspection Points

Inspect the following for Defects, Malfunctions, damage or other signs of wear as per CSA B167-16 Section 6.4.2 Inspection targets for frequent inspection

(a) all operational functions
(b) lines, tanks, valves, pumps, and other parts of Air or Hydraulic systems (for leakage)
(f) limit devices, for proper functioning
(h) brakes

Bridge Inspection - Maximum Safety - "Periodic" Inspection Points

Inspect the following for Defects, Malfunctions, damage or other signs of wear as per CSA B167-16 Section 6.5.2.1 Inspection targets for periodic inspection.

(a) deformed, cracked, or corroded members (e.g., transmission shaft)
(b) missing or visibly loose bolts in the supporting structure
(c) cracked welds
(e) worn, corroded, cracked, or distroted parts such as pins, exposed or open bearings, bushings, shafting, couplings, gears, bumpers, and trolley stops
(f) glazing, scoring, warping, contamination, or wear of electrical and mechanical brakes
(i) evidence of pitting or deterioration of electrical contacts
(j) interference with the free operation of buttons and controls
(k) damaged insulation on the electrical wire, cables, and controls
(l) damaged, deformed, or misaligned festoon system or bus bars.
(m) malfunction of heaters as can be determined without disassembly
(o) worn and/or damaged wheel assemblies
(p) cracked, distorted, or worn guided rollers(e.g., on rack and pinion hoist, on trolleys or bridges)
(t) safeguards and shields for moving equipment
(u) legibility of critical marks and labels (e.g., warning signs, rated load labels)
(v) inadequate oil level

Hoist/Trolley - Minimum Safety - "Frequent" Inspection Points

Inspect the following for Defects, Malfunctions, damage or other signs of wear as per CSA B167-16 Section 6.4.2 Inspection targets for frequent inspection

(a) all operational functions
(b) lines, tanks, valves, pumps, and other parts of Air or Hydraulic systems (for leakage)
(c) hooks, for deformation, wear, or cracking
(d) hook latches, where present
(e) hoist ropes or load chains
(f) limit devices, for proper functioning
(g) function labels for operator control
(h) brakes

Hoist/Trolley - Maximum Safety - "Periodic" Inspection Points

Inspect the following for Defects, Malfunctions, damage or other signs of wear as per CSA B167-16 Section 6.5.2.1 Inspection targets for periodic inspection.

(a) deformed, cracked, or corroded members (e.g., transmission shaft)
(c) cracked welds
(f) glazing, scoring, warping, contamination, or wear of electrical and mechanical brakes
(i) evidence of pitting or deterioration of electrical contacts
(j) interference with the free operation of buttons and controls
(k) damaged insulation on the electrical wire, cables, and controls
(l) damaged, deformed, or misaligned festoon system or bus bars.
(m) malfunction of heaters as can be determined without disassembly
(n) inadequate performance or reliability of the limit switch
(o) worn and/or damaged wheel assemblies
(p) cracked, distorted, or worn guided rollers(e.g., on rack and pinion hoist, on trolleys or bridges)
(q) malfunction controlled lowering motion
(r) wear, cracks, or corrosion of wire rope, load chain, end clamps or rope clips
(s) rope reeving (for compliance with crane Mfg. specs)
(t) safeguards and shields for moving equipment
(u) legibility of critical marks and labels (e.g., warning signs, rated load labels)
(v) inadequate oil level
(w) corroded, cracked, or distorted lower blocks

Preventative Maintenance Inspection Points and 15 Minute Repairs

Bridge - Preventative Maintenance Inspection Points

(a) Lubrication of Grease Nipples & Sensory Check of Oil Viscosity & Condition
(b) Disassembly, Inspection and Measurements of Brakes
(d) Disassembly, Inspection and Measurement of Electrical Contacts, Resistors & Brushes
(e) Cleaning, Inspection and Measurement of Bridge Wheels

Hoist/Trolley - Preventative Maintenance Inspection Points

(a) Lubrication of Grease Nipples & Sensory Check of Oil Viscosity & Condition
(b) Disassembly, Inspection and Measurements of Brakes
(c) Cleaning, Inspection, Measurement & relubrication of drive Chain
(d) Disassembly, Inspection and Measurement of Electrical Contacts, Resistors & Brushes
(e) Cleaning, Inspection and Measurement of Trolley Wheels
